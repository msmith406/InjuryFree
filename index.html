Muscle Imbalances
Muscle Imbalancesclose

Rowing, especially sweeping, can lead to muscle imbalances. Here's some info on some common rowing-induced muscle imbalances and how to fix them.

Gluteal Weakness - AKA You need to work on that butt
Problems this causes: low back pain, knee pain
How to fix it: Romainian Deadlift, Band Good Morning, X-Band Walk, BlockPull/Elevated Deadlift, Kettlebell Swing

Hip Flexor Tightness - Your hips don't like all that sitting
Problems this causes: Pain, pain, pain, inflexibility
How to fix it: Mobility, Single Leg Squats

Thoracic Kyphosis and Internally Rotated Shoulders - Rowing Hunchback
Problems this causes: Inflexibility, pain
How to fix it: Face Pull, YWT Raise, Batwing Row, Pull-Up, Band Pullapart
