Lower Body Strength and Flexibility Quiz

This quiz is designed to determine your current level of lower body strength and overall lower body flexibility. Stretches and strengthening exercises will be suggested based on your results.

1. Stand with your feet together and attempt to bend and touch your toes.

2. Following the picture, while keeping one leg straight on the floor, pull the other leg towards the opposite shoulder.

3. Using the picture as a guide. Lie with one leg bent and, keeping it straight, slowly raise your other leg until it is even with your bent knee. Slowly return it to the floor, still keeping it straight. Repeat 25 times.
